The Certificate, License, Permit (CLP) is a framework commonly used in trade and regulatory environments to manage the necessary authorizations and documents required for various business activities. In the context of the Bangladesh Single Window (BSW), the CLP framework refers to the collection of certificates, licenses, and permits that traders and businesses need to obtain for the import, export, and transit of goods.
